1. Google Business Profile (GBP) üìç

Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business) is your free front door on Google Search and Maps. It allows you to manage how your business appears online, connect with customers through reviews, post updates, and even showcase your products and services directly in search results. Essential for local businesses!

  • Visibility: Appear in local search results and Google Maps.
  • Credibility: Collect and respond to customer reviews.
  • Information Hub: Display hours, contact info, website, and photos.

Action: Claim and optimize your GBP listing to be found by local customers.

4. PageSpeed Insights to Test Your Site Speed ‚ö°

Website speed is no longer just a nice-to-have; it's a critical ranking factor for Google and directly impacts user experience. Google PageSpeed Insights is a free tool that analyzes your website's performance on both mobile and desktop, providing a score and actionable recommendations to make your site faster. Use it regularly to identify and fix bottlenecks.

  • Performance Score: Get an overall rating for your site speed.
  • Actionable Recommendations: Learn exactly how to improve load times.
  • Mobile & Desktop Analysis: See performance across different devices.

Why it's vital: Faster sites lead to lower bounce rates, better SEO, and happier customers.
