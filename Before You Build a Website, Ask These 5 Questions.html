Before You Build: Ask These 5 Essential Questions 💡

Don't jump into website design without a clear strategy. These critical questions will help you build an online presence that truly works for your business.

1. What’s the #1 Goal of My Site? 🎯

Every great website has a primary objective. Are you aiming to generate leads, drive e-commerce sales, provide comprehensive information, or boost brand awareness? Pinpointing your top goal will dictate every design, content, and feature choice, ensuring your website is a strategic asset, not just an online brochure.

Why it matters: A clear goal means a focused, effective website that delivers real results.

2. Who is My Target Customer? 👤

You're building this site for someone! Understanding your target audience—their demographics, pain points, online behavior, and what they hope to gain—is crucial. Your website's design, language, and user experience should resonate directly with them, making it easy and enjoyable for them to interact with your brand.

  • Demographics: Age, location, income, education.
  • Psychographics: Interests, values, lifestyle, pain points.
  • Online Habits: What devices do they use? What content do they consume?

3. Do I Have Branding Ready? 🎨

Your brand is your business's personality. Before building, ensure you have a clear brand message and consistent visual assets. This includes your logo, color palette, typography, and image style. A cohesive brand identity across your website builds trust and recognition, making your site memorable.

🖌️

Checklist:

  • ✅ Defined Brand Message
  • ✅ Finalized Logo & Variations
  • ✅ Established Color Palette
  • ✅ Chosen Fonts
  • ✅ Visual Style Guide (optional but recommended)

Your Brand = Your Promise

It's what people expect when they see your name.

4. What Content Do I Need? ✍️

Content is the fuel for your website. It's not just text; it includes images, videos, testimonials, and calls to action. Plan what information you'll need for each page (e.g., services, products, about us, contact) and ensure it's high-quality, informative, and engaging for both users and search engines. A content audit or strategy can be incredibly helpful here.

Tip: Start outlining key pages and the main points for each. Think about how visuals can enhance your message.

  • 📝

    Page Copy

    Clear, concise, SEO-friendly text for all sections.

  • 📸

    High-Quality Visuals

    Engaging photos, graphics, or illustrations.

  • 🎥

    Videos (Optional)

    Product demos, testimonials, or brand stories.

5. What Makes My Business Stand Out? ✨

In a crowded online space, your unique value proposition (UVP) is what truly sets you apart. Clearly define what makes your business special, why customers should choose you over competitors, and what specific problems you solve. Your website is the perfect platform to highlight these strengths and articulate your competitive edge.

Your UVP should be woven into your messaging across the entire site!
